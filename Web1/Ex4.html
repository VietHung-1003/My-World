From Garden to Plate

A potager is a French term for an ornamental vegetable or kitchen garden. Often flowers (edibla and non-edible) and herbs are planted with the vegetables to enhance the garden's beauty. The goal is to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing

What to Plant

Plants are chosen as much for their functionality as for their color and form. Many are trained to grow upward. A well-designed potager can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintanance. Potagers can disguise their function of providung for a home in a wide array of forms - from the carefree style of the cottage garden to the formality of a knot garden.
